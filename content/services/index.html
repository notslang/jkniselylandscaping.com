Our Services

Services

J Knisely Landscaping offers an array of landscape maintenance services. Whether you are interested in just having your lawn cut on a weekly or bi-weekly basis or you’d like to sign up for our complete maintenance package, we can make sure that your property gets the attention it deserves. A typical maintenance package includes Spring and Fall Cleanups, Edging and Mulching of Beds in the spring, Lawn Cutting, and bed weeding and shrub pruning. We also offer lawn dethatching and aeration, over-seeding in the fall, and annual flower plantings.

As fall turns to winter, the thought of snow management comes to mind. Our services include snow plowing, shoveling of walkways / garage doors, and driveway sanding. The weather can be exceptionally bad during the winter months in New England with snow totals in excess of 20inches in one storm! During winters of high snow totals we offer snow bank removal and snow relocation.
